Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
ExpressDeal is intended for users 18 years of age or older. By using this service, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use ExpressDeal:

— Email address (for account creation and login)
— Invoice data including client names, service descriptions, and amounts
— Your name and tax ID as entered in invoices

2. How We Use Your Information

— To provide invoice generation and PDF download services
— To send invoices to your clients via email on your behalf
— To maintain your private invoice history
— We do not sell your data to any third party. Ever.

3. Data Security

Your password is encrypted using industry-standard hashing. Invoice data is private to your account — no other user can access your invoices. All connections are secured via HTTPS.

4. Third Party Services

We use Gmail SMTP (Google) to send emails on your behalf. Google's privacy policy applies to this service: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to:
— Access your personal data
— Request correction or deletion of your data
— Object to processing of your data
— Request a copy of your data

7. Age Restriction

ExpressDeal is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Contact

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify registered users by email of any significant changes. Continued use of ExpressDeal after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
